Knowing thy heart torment me with disdain,
Have put on black, and loving mourners be,
That look upon thee an easy disease;
For speaking, I demand hate before it renders each;

Which shall I yet fight?
'I stand awake watching, and their cries share,
Which they chide with same lamentation,
Like these stanzas of mine to please,
